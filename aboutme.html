-About: Angela Kihiko-

Angela hails from the beautiful state of Kentucky. She is an alumna of the prestigious Spelman College in Atlanta, GA, where she earned her degree in Computer Science.

Along the way, she delved into the intricate beauty of the Japanese language, earning a minor degree. Her passion for exploring different cultures led her to fascinating experiences, including a transformative study abroad journey in Japan and a domestic exchange program at Barnard College.

During her academic journey, she had the incredible opportunity to participate in a Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) at the University of Miami, where she honed her research skills and expanded her knowledge in the field of music computational science.

Additionally, she ventured into the magical world of entertainment as a creative software developer intern for Disney, where she contributed to crafting innovative and immersive digital experiences.

Eager to become an educator and share her knowledge and enthusiasm, Angela became TEFL Certified and embarked on a teaching adventure in Daegu, South Korea, for a fulfilling year at Daegu Foreign Language High School. Her time there not only enriched her life but also allowed her to connect with diverse minds and cultures.

Beyond her academic and professional pursuits, Angela finds solace and inspiration in the arts. She is a flutist, poet, and philosopher. With a deep commitment to well-being, Angela practices mindfulness and yoga daily. These practices have become an integral part of her life, guiding her toward balance and serenity amidst life's challenges.

In every endeavor, she is driven by the belief that technology, when harnessed thoughtfully, can break barriers, uplift communities, and contribute positively to mental health.

Angela's mission is to facilitate a future where everyone, regardless of their background, can embrace the digital world with confidence and use it as a catalyst for personal development and societal change.
